# Simulation Backends

```{warning}
This chapter is currently a plan/outline, and may not represent the exact implementation once completed. 
```

`LoQS` uses a variety of third-party packages to do the underlying physical circuit simulation.
There are three types of backends: circuits, models, and states.
In this chapter, we will look at what each backend is responsible for and what third-party simulation packages have been integrated so far.
